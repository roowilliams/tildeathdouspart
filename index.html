WTF IS ABERGAVENNY

A pretty, little welsh town, about 20 mins from Lydia’s house and the wedding field. It has cute coffee shops/art galleries, bakeries, award-winning, sausage-making butchers, historical ruins, hikes and weekend craft/food markets etc.

AND HOW DO I GET THERE

To get to Abergavenny from London, either take a train to Newport, South Wales and get a short cab or change at Newport for Abergavenny station. Uber has not yet reached rural Wales, so here are some local taxi numbers: Dragon (Newport), Lewis (Abergavenny)
